## 📌 Project Overview
This project was developed as part of the Digital Literacy course to understand and apply essential digital skills. It focuses on building a professional online presence, practicing safe internet usage, and improving digital communication skills.

---

## 📂 Project Structure

### 🔹 Task 1 – Infographic
Designed a digital literacy infographic using Canva, covering topics like digital tools, online safety, professional presence, and email etiquette.

### 🔹 Task 2 – Digital Portfolio
Created and updated professional profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Stack Overflow to build a strong online presence.

### 🔹 Task 3 – Platforms
Solved a beginner-level coding problem on HackerRank and created a Google Form quiz to spread digital literacy awareness.

### 🔹 Task 4 – Email Etiquette
Drafted professional emails and created a social media do’s and don’ts checklist to promote responsible communication.

### 🔹 Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
Prepared a cybercrime case study and a prevention checklist to understand online threats and safety measures.
